The Great Pindar Svetlin Nakov

Founder and great master of the Software Illuminati Nakov ‘s dreams go beyond every man’s imagination. His goal is to make Bulgaria the most IT advanced country in the world and use that to take over the world bringing The New World Order. Svetlin is powerful, arrogant and crushes his enemies source code to bits. If there is something this titan can’t do, that's finding the ANY key.

Angel Georgiev

The smartest man in the organization and Nakov’s right hand. Angel's IQ is so high that few times in his life he managed to win an argument against a compilator. To be honest we've never seen him do anything productive for the organization besides this one time he succeeded hiding all of Nakov's weed in the basement from the policeman who visited the building.

Bogomil Dimitrov

Bogomil is SoftUni Illuminati's boy for everything. He brings coffee to the whole office in the morning and massages Nakov’s feet when asked to. Mr. Dimitrov is probably the only one who realizes how stupid the idea behind the organization is, but he accepted Nakov's offer to join the council because he needed the money. Later on he found out that the council meetings can be great resource for his upcoming book "Software Engineers: The Stupidity".

Borislav Hristov

Borislav is SoftUni Illuminati's network engineer a.k.a the man responsible for everything that happens outside the building. He knows the browser history of every single member in the organization, that's why everyone panics when he is out of his office (a 2 by 2 meters dark cubicle). Someone once told us that he got a message from alien civilization and wrote it down on a piece of paper, but then he got distracted by the new Justin Bieber video and lost the note.

Deyn Dachev

Dido is one of the craziest people you'll ever get the chance to meet. Nakov hired him to be SoftUni Illuminati's activity manager (The only line of code Dido can write is: Console.WriteLine("Hello world"); ). And by activity manager we mean gathering the whole building in the gym and playing Ping-Pong with Nakov while the rest of the members play Angry Birds on their smartphones.

Hristo Chakarov

Nobody knows what is he doing in the council not even Nakov. All we know about him is that he spends most of the time coding but no one knows exactly what. He doesn't speak often in meetings, but when he does, every single one of his sentences shows that he is an IT specialist: everything Hristo says is technically correct, but it's of no use to anyone. We heard that someone saw him eating glue.

Ivan Yonkov

Yonkov is the creator of the organization's website (No! Not this one ! Wait.. what? Illuminati confirmed!) . He is also known for being intense keyboard ninja. Always ready to argue on the forums for basically everything. There is one day of the year when Ivan organizes debate on different Illuminati topics and the only one who shows up to work that day is Angel (We were told that they almost killed each other over an argument once).

Ivan Vankov

He is like one of the Jehovah's Witnesses but instead of going to people's houses asking to talk with them about Jesus he goes to people's offices in the building asking them if they have time to talk about why Linux is better than Windows.
